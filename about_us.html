How it all started
Searching for scalability in near future

How can an expatriate person, as a result of his work, search in the suburbs of Cairo for the bus station he needs to reach in order to reach his destination?
Certainly, if you have encountered this problem, you will know how difficult it is. The only solution is to wait for each bus until you see the names of the stations it passes through, which is of course exhausting. Hence the solution come..
The previous example is a real situation that a member of the Ashy team was exposed to, who decided as soon as possible to try to find a solution to this problem, and indeed we created Ashy..
The Ashy application is a web application through which you can know the number of the bus that you need to ride until you reach your specified destination, and it also gives you the nearest station nearby that the bus passes through
